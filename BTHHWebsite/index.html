Infant Mortality

  • In the United States, for every white infant that dies, 2 black infants die
  • In New Jersey, where we plan to launch the app, 5 black infants die for every white infant
  • This disparity is a result of black and brown communities’ lack of access to accurate information as well as their frequent mistreatment in a hospital / doctor setting
  • This is a systematic problem that stretches across the entire country and requires easily accessible information to be rapidly dispersed to all pregnant mothers




Company Mission

Our goal is to develop an application that uses a machine-learning algorithm to analyze past infant mortality data to provide mothers with insight into their pregnancy’s potential health consequences. Although this app would help all expecting mothers, it is primarily targeted towards women of color to close the health disparity between black and white communities. Minority groups have been historically mistreated in hospitals due to inherent biases, and we are aiming to provide a systematic solution. Our app would empower mothers so they can be fully aware of their own healthcare needs and demand for catered treatment to prevent adverse health effects during the pregnancy and beyond.
